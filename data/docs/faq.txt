Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What payment methods do you accept?
A: We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. For enterprise customers, we also offer invoice billing with NET 30 terms.

Q: Do you offer international shipping?
A: Yes, we ship to over 100 countries worldwide. Shipping times vary by destination, typically 7-14 business days for international orders. Customs fees may apply and are the responsibility of the customer.

Q: How do I track my order?
A: Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can also track your order status by logging into your account and viewing your order history.

Q: What is your privacy policy?
A: We take privacy seriously. We never sell your personal information to third parties. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. See our full privacy policy at www.company.com/privacy

Q: Can I change or cancel my order?
A: Orders can be modified or cancelled within 2 hours of placement. After that, the order enters our fulfillment process and cannot be changed. However, you can always return items per our return policy.

Q: Do you offer bulk discounts?
A: Yes! Orders of 10+ units qualify for volume pricing. Enterprise customers can contact sales@company.com for custom quotes and dedicated account management.

Q: What about technical support?
A: Technical support is available 24/7 via live chat, email, or phone. Premium customers receive priority support with guaranteed response times under 1 hour.
