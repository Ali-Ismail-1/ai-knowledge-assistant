Product User Guide - SmartWidget Pro

Getting Started:
The SmartWidget Pro is our flagship product designed for productivity enthusiasts. It features AI-powered automation, cloud sync, and advanced analytics.

Key Features:
- Voice command integration with 95% accuracy
- Real-time collaboration with up to 50 users
- End-to-end encryption for data security
- 24/7 customer support
- Mobile app available on iOS and Android

Setup Instructions:
1. Unbox your SmartWidget Pro and connect to power
2. Download the companion app from your device's app store
3. Create an account or sign in with existing credentials
4. Follow the in-app setup wizard
5. Customize your preferences in Settings

Troubleshooting:
If your device won't connect to WiFi, ensure you're using a 2.4GHz network. The SmartWidget Pro does not support 5GHz networks at this time.

For battery issues, try a full power cycle by holding the power button for 10 seconds.

Warranty:
All SmartWidget Pro devices come with a 2-year manufacturer warranty covering defects in materials and workmanship.
