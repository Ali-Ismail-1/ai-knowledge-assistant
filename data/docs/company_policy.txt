Company Return Policy

All products purchased from our company can be returned within 30 days of the original purchase date. Items must be in their original condition with all tags attached.

Return Process:
1. Contact customer service to initiate a return
2. Pack the item securely in its original packaging
3. Use the prepaid shipping label provided
4. Drop off at any authorized shipping location

Refund Timeline:
Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive the returned item. The refund will be issued to the original payment method.

Exceptions:
- Final sale items cannot be returned
- Custom or personalized items are not eligible for return
- Items damaged due to misuse are not covered

For questions, contact support@company.com or call 1-800-RETURNS.
